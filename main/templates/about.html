About the Conference

Why STICONF 2026?

A strategic national and international platform to bridge the persistent technology divide between research outputs and industry adoption.

Background & Rationale

Science, technology, and innovation (STI) remain critical drivers of economic transformation, industrial competitiveness, and sustainable development. Despite Nigeria's growing pool of research institutions, universities, and innovation centres, a persistent technology divide exists between research outputs and industry adoption.

Many research findings fail to transition into market-ready products, services, or scalable solutions due to weak collaboration frameworks, limited commercialization pathways, inadequate funding mechanisms, and policy disconnects.

STICONF 2026 is conceived as a strategic national and international platform to address these gaps by fostering structured, outcome-oriented engagement between research institutions, industry players, policymakers, investors, and development partners. The conference aligns with Nigeria's aspirations for industrialisation, knowledge-based growth, and inclusive innovation.

Aim

To catalyse effective and sustainable linkages between research institutions and industry for accelerated technology transfer, innovation commercialization, and socio-economic development.

Objectives

  • Assess existing gaps and barriers in research–industry technology linkages
  • Showcase demand-driven research, indigenous technologies, and scalable innovations
  • Promote policy, regulatory, and financing frameworks that support technology transfer
  • Facilitate partnerships among academia, industry, government, and investors
  • Generate actionable outcomes including MOUs, pilot projects, and policy recommendations
